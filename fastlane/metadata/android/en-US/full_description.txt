Paperless Scanner is the mobile companion for your Paperless-ngx installation. Scan documents on the go and upload them directly to your paperless archive.

FEATURES:

- Smart Scanning: Automatic edge detection and perspective correction
- Multi-Page Support: Combine multiple pages into a single PDF
- Gallery Import: Import existing photos as documents
- Direct Upload: Seamless integration with Paperless-ngx
- Biometric Security: Login with fingerprint or Face ID
- Home Screen Widget: Quick access to scanning
- Offline Queue: Documents upload when connection is available

REQUIREMENTS:

This app requires your own Paperless-ngx installation. Paperless-ngx is an open-source document management system that you can self-host.

More information: https://docs.paperless-ngx.com

PRIVACY:

- No ads
- No tracking
- Your documents stay on your server
- Credentials are stored securely on device

Open Source: https://github.com/napoleonmm83/paperless-scanner
